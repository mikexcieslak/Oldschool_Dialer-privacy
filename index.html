Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Oldschool Dialer app (the “App”) handles user data and permissions. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Purpose of the App

The App is designed as a functional rotary phone dialer. Its primary purpose is to allow users to dial phone numbers using a circular dial interface and place phone calls directly from the App.

2. Phone Call Permission (CALL_PHONE)

The App requires access to the device’s phone calling functionality (android.permission.CALL_PHONE) in order to place phone calls initiated by the user.

Phone calls are placed only after the user:

  • Manually dials a phone number using the rotary dial interface
  • Explicitly presses the Call button

The App does not place calls automatically, in the background, or without clear user interaction.

3. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, process, or transmit:

  • Phone numbers
  • Call history or call logs
  • Call duration or metadata
  • Contacts or contact information
  • Personal or sensitive user data

All phone call actions are handled directly by the device’s operating system.

4. Data Sharing

The App does not share any phone-related data with third parties.

The App does not use phone functionality for analytics, advertising, profiling, or tracking purposes.

5. User Control

Users can deny or revoke the phone call permission at any time through their device settings.

If the phone call permission is denied, the App will open the system dialer instead of placing the call directly.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party services that access phone functionality or phone-related data.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the App’s behavior, you may contact the developer through the contact information provided on the app’s Google Play or App Store listing.
